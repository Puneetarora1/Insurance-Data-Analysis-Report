# Insurance Data Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
Developed an interactive **Power BI dashboard** to analyze insurance policy performance, claim amounts, claim status, customer activity, and settlement trends. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key KPIs, compare policy types, and identify claim patterns for better decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Power BI** – Dashboard Development & Data Visualization
- **Power Query** – Data Cleaning & Transformation
- **DAX** – Measures & Calculated Columns
- **MS SQL** – Data Source
- **Interactive Visuals** – Cards, Slicers, Pie Charts, Matrix Table, Bar Charts, Line Charts
- **Power BI Service** - Publish

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Premium Amount collected is **$5.98M**, while the total Claim Amount is **$16.9M**.
- **Travel Insurance** generates the highest Premium (**$2.5M**) and also records the highest Claim Amount (**$7.1M**).
- **Adults** contribute the highest Premium (**$3.1M**) and Claim Amount (**$8.8M**) among all age groups.
- **73.09%** of policies are **Active**, while **26.91%** are **Inactive**, indicating strong policy retention.
- Among inactive customers, **43.5%** could not claim their policy.
- **22.6%** of insurance claims are currently **Pending**.
